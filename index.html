Who We Are

A distribution partner built for the Filipino pantry

Tiptop Distribution Inc. connects trusted condiment and sauce brands with the sari-sari stores, groceries, and food service businesses that keep every Filipino table full.

We handle the logistics — storage, order fulfillment, and delivery — so our brand partners can focus on what they do best, and our retail partners always have stock on hand.

  • Reliable fulfillment — consistent, on-schedule deliveries across our coverage area.
  • Proper storage & handling — temperature-appropriate warehousing for every product line.
  • Retailer support — flexible order sizes for stores of every scale.
